Hey! My name's Drew Segovia. Thanks for coming to my site, I hope you'll soon reach out and schedule a first session with me, and see for yourself what I'm' all about, but until then, I'll do my best to tell you. I started tutoring in early 2017. Yes, it's a business, but it's also personal for me.

Not too long ago, I was a student preparing to go through the SAT process. It was about midway through my sophomore year, so I had some time, but I started looking into what the next year or so would entail. I went on Reddit, I talked to my friends, I talked to their siblings and what I found out was that everyone and their mother was taking SAT prep classes. I looked into some of those classes, and even the cheapest of those options were running at like 800 or 900 bucks. With prices like that, those options were off the table for me, and that hurt, because I knew I was missing out on something that could really help.

Equal opportunity in education is one of pillars of our society, but prices like that block bright kids from access to the same resources available to the wealthy.

That's why I offer private lessons, either over Zoom or in person when possible. You might think that I lose soemthing withiut acces to the same resources as big tutoring centers, but access to public material on the internet is all you really need to digest the stuff that gets taught up through senior year. With that, I can offer a similar service to any at a fraction of the cost
