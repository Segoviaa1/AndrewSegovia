I'm also a Midshipman first class in the Boston NROTC Consortium pursuing a commision as a pilot into the U.S. Navy.

Through positions within Boston NROTC, Boston College Mock Trial, I have experiences leading teams that range in size from 4 individuals all the way up to 120.

I'm glad you found my little site! Whatever brought you here, I hope you'll take the time to read through some of my experiences. At the end of it all, please reach out, or drop me a follow on social media. Contact info linked at the bottom of each page.
